## Methodology
-Downloaded the Homo sapiens BRCA1 transcript variant 1 mRNA sequence in FASTA format from NCBI.
-Opened the NCBI BLASTN tool.
-Pasted the human BRCA1 sequence as the query.
-Performed separate BLAST searches by filtering for individual organisms.
-Recorded the top BLAST hit for each organism.
-Compared Query Coverage, Percent Identity, and E-value.

## Interpretation
-The BLAST analysis showed that the BRCA1 gene is highly conserved among mammals.
-The chimpanzee sequence displayed the highest similarity to the human BRCA1 sequence, followed by the gorilla sequence. The mouse sequence showed lower sequence identity than the primates, reflecting greater evolutionary distance while still maintaining significant similarity.
-The low E-values obtained in the analysis indicate that these sequence matches are statistically significant and unlikely to have occurred by chance.
